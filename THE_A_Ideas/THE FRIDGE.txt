PART-1: THE OPENING
Mrs. Gayatri reports a foul smell coming from her neighbor Anjali's house.
The police arrive and find Anjali dead in her apartment.
Injured head, lying face first in front of an open refrigerator. 
The door is closed from inside and the apartment is on the 13th floor.
The CCTV shows no one entering or leaving the house from the day of death till the police arrive.
Post-mortem reports say that Anajli died 10 days back.
She had not paid her electricity dues which caused a power cut which made the fridge expel smell.
Police find reports of her anger issues and that she was an alcoholic who also had a case of DUI.
No reports of her family were found and due to the huge number of cases the inspector wanted to shut it ASAP.
Hence, the police closed the case as Anjali was blackout drunk and she put herself inside the fridge and died of suffocation.
